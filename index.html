Eurovision Song Contest 2025

The Eurovision Song Contest, often known simply as Eurovision, is an international song competition organised annually by the European Broadcasting Union (EBU) among its members.
Each participating broadcaster submits an original song representing its country to be performed and broadcast live to all of thembr
via the Eurovision and Euroradio networks ,and then casts votes for the other countries' songs to determine a winner.

In this site I will rate my top 4 songs for the Eurovision Song contest 2025,
I will share info on the song, which artist representing each country and a link to each song.


Sweeden

Sweden will be represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2025 with the song "Bara bada bastu",
written by Anderz Wrethov, Axel Ahman, Jakob Norrgard, Kevin Holmstrom, Kristofer Strandberg, and Robert Skowronski,
performed by Ahman, Norrgard, and Holmstrom as KAJ.

Austria

Austria will be represented at the Eurovision Song Contest 2025 with the song "Wasted Love",
written by Johannes Pietsch, Teodora Špirić, and Thomas Thurner, and performed by Pietsch himself under his stage name JJ.

France

France is entering Eurovision 2025 in Basel in style, as always. This year, they are represented by Louane, an artist who is already a big name,
competing with the impressive ballad “Maman.” Since 2023,
France has returned to internal selections, and Louane was chosen through this process as well.

Israel

Israel unveiled its official song entry for the 2025 Eurovision Song Contest, an emotional power ballad titled “New Day Will Rise.”
The track is performed by Yuval Raphael, who will represent Israel at this year's competition
